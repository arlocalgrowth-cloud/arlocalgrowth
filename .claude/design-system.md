\# Design System



\## Design Philosophy



The website should feel:



\* Premium

\* Modern

\* Minimal

\* Trustworthy

\* Professional

\* Fast

\* Clear



The visual impression should be closer to a modern SaaS company than a traditional marketing agency.



Avoid:



\* Generic agency designs

\* Corporate stock photos

\* Excessive gradients

\* Heavy shadows

\* Visual clutter



\---



\# Design Inspirations



Use inspiration from:



\* Google

\* Stripe

\* Linear

\* Vercel

\* Notion

\* Modern components from 21st.dev



Do not directly copy any brand.



\---



\# Layout Principles



Large whitespace.



Comfortable spacing.



Clear hierarchy.



Consistent section rhythm.



Sections should breathe.



Avoid dense layouts.

\## Usage Rules



Blue:

Primary actions and links.



Green:

Success indicators and growth metrics.



Yellow:

Highlights and small accents.



Red:

Warnings and micro accents only.



Never create colorful sections.



Use colors with restraint.

\## Body Text



Readable.



Professional.



Not overly large.



Use line-height generously.



\---



\# Border Radius



Cards:



16px–24px



Buttons:



12px–16px



Inputs:



12px–16px



\---



\# Shadows



Soft shadows only.



Example:



Small:

0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)



Medium:

0 8px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)



Avoid dramatic shadows.



\---



\# Buttons



Primary:



Blue background



White text



Subtle hover



\---



Secondary:



White background



Border



Dark text



\---



\# Cards



All cards should have:



\* Rounded corners

\* Soft shadow

\* Light border

\* Subtle hover animation



Cards should feel elevated but not floating excessively.



\---



\# Hero Dashboard



Hero visual should consist of:



Three floating metric cards.



Card 1:



Google Maps Ranking



Position 12 → Position 3



Card 2:



Google Reviews



48 → 127



Card 3:



Monthly Leads



14 → 39



Requirements:



\* Smooth floating animation

\* Framer Motion

\* Number transitions

\* Soft shadows

\* Glass effect kept subtle



\---



\# Animations



Animations should communicate quality.



Use:



\* Fade in

\* Slide up

\* Floating motion

\* Counter animations



Avoid:



\* Spin

\* Bounce

\* Flashing effects

\* Excessive motion



\---



\# Icons



Use:



Lucide Icons



Style:



Minimal

Outlined

Modern



\---



\# Responsive Design



Mobile first.



Must look excellent on:



\* Mobile

\* Tablet

\* Laptop

\* Desktop



No horizontal scrolling.



\---



\# Images



Avoid stock photography.



Prefer:



\* Interface visuals

\* Abstract growth illustrations

\* Maps-inspired graphics

\* Dashboard visuals



\---



\# Visual Tone



The website should communicate:



Trust

Growth

Professionalism

Local Expertise

Clarity



The visitor should feel:



"This company understands local businesses and knows how to generate results."
